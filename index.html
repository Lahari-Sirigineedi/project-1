Araku Valley


The Beaten Path In Andhra Pradesh

Araku Valley is in the heart of Andhra Pradesh which is also called as the OOTY of andhra. It is is a hill station
in Visakhapatnam district in the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh. It's surrounded by high
mountains, lush green forests, foggy clouds, and a pleasing climate.

Know more about Araku Valley:

Best Time to Visit Araku Valley

Prefer visiting Araku Valley during winters which is from the months of December to February. One can enjoy the best of sightseeing despite the fact that Araku Valley looks stunning in freezing temperatures that goes down to about 5-degree Celsius.

Best Places to visit

Chapari Waterfalls:

It is also known as the Dumbriguda Falls.It is one of the most wonderful place to visit in Araku Valley.

Araku Tribal Museum:

Tribal Museum is one of the most popular place created for making people aware of the Araku Valley’s lifestyle and culture by taking them through its historical past.

Bongulo Chicken:

One of the famous dish of araku valley is bongulo chicken, it is famous for being prepared with a unique cooking method.
